About

  • Restron is a platform that connects people who are passionate about food and interested in buying or selling home-cooked food.

  • It provides a platform for expression and recognition for home chefs, who can now share their mouthwatering delicacies with not just their family members but also their neighbours

  • For a foody, if you are wondering where to get authentic homemade Bihari Litti Chokha, Kerala Puttu Kadala, Andhra Gottu Vankaya, Rajasthani Daal Baati Churma or Gujarati Undhyu - FoodyBuddy is the solution for you. You just want simple Dal Chawal, Roti Subzi and Idli Sambar? We have you covered as well.

  • Currently, FoodyBuddy can be used by residents of large apartment communities in Bangalore to buy and sell home-cooked food within their own community and neighbouring communities.
